In 2024, Vidyashilp Academy evolved into Vidyashilp International School — a bold step reflecting our renewed vision for world-class education. This rebrand marks not just a change in name, but a deepened commitment to preparing students for an interconnected, rapidly changing world.

Rooted in over 15 years of academic excellence, we blend traditional Indian values with modern pedagogical innovation. Our approach nurtures curious minds, fosters critical thinking, and empowers every student to become a confident global citizen.
