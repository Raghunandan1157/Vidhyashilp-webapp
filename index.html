About Vidhyashilp Academy

Vidhyashilp Academy is a premier educational institution located in Bengaluru, Karnataka, committed to nurturing young minds through a blend of academic excellence and holistic development. With a strong foundation built over 25 years, we offer CBSE and ICSE curricula designed to prepare students for a globally competitive future.

Our modern infrastructure, state-of-the-art laboratories, expansive sports facilities, and dedicated faculty of over 80 professionals create an environment where every student thrives. We believe in leveraging technology to bridge the gap between school and home, ensuring parents stay connected with their child's academic journey every step of the way.
