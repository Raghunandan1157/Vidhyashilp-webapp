About Us

Discover the journey of Vidyashilp International School — from our humble beginnings to becoming a beacon of quality education in Haveri

Our Story

Founded in 2009 as Vidyashilp Academy, our school began with a simple yet powerful mission — to bring quality, value-driven education to the children of Haveri and surrounding communities. What started with a handful of students and a passionate team of educators has grown into one of the most respected educational institutions in the region.

In 2024, we proudly rebranded as Vidyashilp International School, reflecting our expanded vision of preparing students for the global stage. This transformation was guided by the Navachetana Foundation, which continues to invest in educational innovation, teacher training, and infrastructure development.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing and intellectually stimulating environment where every student can discover their potential, develop critical thinking skills, and grow into responsible global citizens equipped to make a meaningful impact.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the leading institution in North Karnataka for holistic education that seamlessly blends academic rigor with character development, technological innovation, and a deep respect for cultural values.
