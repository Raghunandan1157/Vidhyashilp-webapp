OUR CURRICULUM

Curriculum Overview

At Vidyashilp International School, we follow the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) curriculum enriched with modern pedagogical practices. Our approach goes beyond textbooks—integrating technology, experiential learning, and skill-based education.

Our curriculum emphasizes conceptual clarity, interdisciplinary connections, and practical application of knowledge, ensuring that our students are prepared for the challenges of the 21st century.
