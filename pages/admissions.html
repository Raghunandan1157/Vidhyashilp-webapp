Admission Process

A simple four-step journey to becoming part of our school community.

1

Online Registration

Fill out the online application form with student and parent details. Submit required documents digitally.

2

Assessment & Interaction

Students undergo an age-appropriate assessment. Parents meet with school counselors for an interaction session.

3

Document Verification

Submit original documents for verification. Our team reviews the application and assessment results.

4

Admission Confirmation

Receive the admission offer. Complete fee payment and collect the welcome kit and school uniform.
